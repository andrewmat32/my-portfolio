01/About

A full-stack engineer with a designer's eye

I'm a Full Stack Developer who builds web platforms end to end. For around five years I've shipped travel-booking platforms for tour operators and agencies across the Netherlands, Switzerland and Germany, owning everything from the Node.js back-end to the front-end UI. I'm grounded in core JavaScript and engineering fundamentals — happy to build custom from scratch — and just as fluent with frameworks and CMS (Vue, ApostropheCMS) when they're the right fit. My background in Automation & Applied Informatics is the engineering discipline I bring to every build.

Today I'm the sole developer of the Victoury platform: a marketing CMS and a travel-booking minisite with a large library of custom widgets. Before that I was a core developer on diving and safari sites like Diving World, building booking engines (pricing, cabins, flights and excursions), search, multi-step forms and reusable widget libraries.

I care about the fundamentals users feel but never see: accessibility (WCAG, automated Pa11y checks), performance, SEO and structured data, GDPR-compliant forms, and code other developers can maintain with confidence.

  • Design systems at scaleReusable widgets, tokens and themes that keep many sites consistent.
  • Performance-mindedLazy loading, lean token-driven CSS, fast first paint on any device.
  • SEO & structured dataJSON-LD, sitemaps and semantic markup that help products rank.
  • Accessibility-firstARIA, keyboard nav, contrast and reduced-motion, verified with a11y scans.
